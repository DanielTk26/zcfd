For Donors:

For The Needy:

If you are a donor, type in your details

in the given format....

Location(State,distict), Name, Age, Ph.Number,

previous donation date. And then click

on the post button and your details will

be posted accordingly. If You want to see

your post just click on the posting box again.

Thank You !!!

If you need a blood donor in your area,

then just type in your location

(State,District) and you will see

all the available donors in your area.

Then just click on any available donor

and his required details will appear on

the text bar. You can then contact

the donor using his Ph.Number.
